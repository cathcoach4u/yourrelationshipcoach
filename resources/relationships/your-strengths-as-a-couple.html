Your Strengths as a Couple

What you focus on grows. When you notice strengths, you create more of them.

🌟
My Strengths
What I bring to this relationship

Reflect

What do I do well in this relationship?

Discover

What strengths do others notice in me? (e.g. patience, humour, loyalty, empathy, directness)

Use it this week

If your strength is patience, take the lead in slowing things down during a tense moment.

💚
Our Strengths
What we are strong at together

Reflect

What do we handle well as a team?

Discover

When have we been at our best together? What made that possible?

Use it this week

Divide a task this week based on who is naturally better at it. Play to your strengths as a team.

⭐
Partner’s Strengths
What I see and value in you

Reflect

What does my partner do well in this relationship?

Discover

What three strengths do I most admire in my partner?

Use it this week

After a repair moment, lean on your partner’s strength. If writing is easier than talking, write a note.

Strengths-based conversations

  • I noticed you really listened to me tonight. That meant a lot.
  • You handled that situation with the kids so calmly. I appreciate that.
  • One of the things I value about us is how we can laugh even when things are hard.

Try this

Each partner writes down three strengths they see in the other and shares them. Then discuss: how could we use these strengths more intentionally this week?
