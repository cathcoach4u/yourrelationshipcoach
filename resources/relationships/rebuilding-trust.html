Rebuilding Trust

A structured approach to healing and rebuilding your relationship after betrayal.

Phase 1
🖟
Safety and Stability
Immediate safety and crisis management. Daily structured check-ins create a safe space for both partners to express feelings and acknowledge the breach.
Frequency
Daily check-ins
Phase 2
🤝
Understanding and Accountability
The partner who breached trust takes full responsibility, offering sincere and specific apologies. Both partners express emotions, focusing on feelings rather than stories.
Frequency
Several times per week
Phase 3
🌿
Learning and Restoration
Self-reflection on personal growth and understanding of behaviour. Concrete, measurable actions to rebuild trust. Demonstrating commitment to change.
Frequency
Weekly
Phase 4
🌿
Reconnection and Growth
Transitioning from frequent structured dialogues to more organic, supportive conversations. The relationship heals and strengthens over time.
Frequency
Monthly or as needed

Key Elements of the Process

🕰️
Timing Check
Always ask if it is a good time to talk. Either partner can decline and reschedule, no questions asked.
📊
Safety Number
A rating from 0 to 10 to gauge emotional safety and progress. Even small increases (e.g. moving from −5 to −1) are significant.
💬
Acknowledgment and Requests
Acknowledge when you felt safest. Make specific, tangible requests that can be fulfilled within 24 to 48 hours.
🧎
Managing Emotions
The process is structured to prevent emotional outbursts and keep conversations focused and constructive.

Remember

Small shifts matter. Moving even a single point on the safety scale is progress worth celebrating. Trust is rebuilt through consistent, small actions over time, not grand gestures. Every structured conversation is a step forward.
